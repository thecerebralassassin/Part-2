Dondo Water is a nonprofit organization working to provide clean, safe, and sustainable water solutions to rural communities across South Africa and beyond.

Together, We Can End the Water Crisis in South Africa

Since our founding, Dondo Water has been driven by one bold goal: ending the water crisis in South Africa and globally. Through local partnerships, innovative technologies, and donor support, we are making meaningful progress every day.

Dondo Water’s Impact in South Africa

  • Water Projects Funded: 55
  • Provinces Served: Limpopo, KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng
  • People Impacted: 20 million

Our Promise to You

We promise that 100% of public donations go directly to funding water projects in South Africa. Our operational costs are covered by a separate group of private donors, ensuring all contributions provide clean water.
