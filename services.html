Core Services

💧 Water Access Initiatives: Dondo Water designs and implements water delivery systems, such as boreholes, wells, and rainwater harvesting systems, in rural communities.

🔹 Water Purification & Filtration: We provide and install affordable, low-maintenance water purification systems to ensure that every drop is safe for consumption.

📚 Community Education & Training: We conduct workshops and awareness programs on water conservation, hygiene, and sanitation, empowering local communities to take charge of their water needs.

🤝 Partnerships with Local Governments & Organizations: By collaborating with local and international stakeholders, we help create sustainable water infrastructure in underserved areas.
